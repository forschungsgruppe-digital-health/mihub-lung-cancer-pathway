### 1. Intended use

The BPMN models in this repository are a **research, education, and
interoperability-reference artifact** produced in the MiHUB project (TU Dresden /
Forschungsgruppe Digital Health). They are intended for purposes such as: documenting
and discussing the lung-cancer patient pathway, teaching, and serving as input to
downstream technical work (e.g. FHIR / terminology specification, synthetic-data
generation, process analysis).

### 2. Not for clinical use

These models are **not clinically validated** and are **not** intended for use in
direct patient care or for clinical decision-making. They are **not medical advice**,
**not a substitute** for professional medical judgment, diagnosis, or treatment, and
do **not** establish or represent a standard of care. They must **not** be used to
make or support decisions about an individual patient without independent clinical
validation and a regulatory assessment by the using party.

### 3. Not a medical device

The authors assign these models **no medical intended purpose** within the meaning of
Art. 2(1) of Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (Medical Device Regulation, MDR). They are a
static, non-executable process description that does not by itself drive, control, or
influence a medical device, and they are **not** provided or CE-marked as a medical
device. Any party that repurposes them in a way that gives them a medical intended
purpose assumes the corresponding regulatory responsibility.

The models are provided **"as is" and "as available"**, without warranties of any
